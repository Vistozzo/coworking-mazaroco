The Space

Coworking Mazaroco is a small space: 3 fixed desks and 10 shared desks.
There are moments of focus and moments of conversation.
If you are looking for absolute library-like silence, this might not be your place.

We are open from 8:00 to 18:00, with the possibility of extended hours upon request.
